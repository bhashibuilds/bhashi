Medtora — AI-Powered Mentorship for Aspiring Medical Professionals

End-to-End Product Design, AI Integration, Full-Stack Development

Case Study • 2025

Setting the Scene

First-generation and underrepresented students often face significant barriers in accessing mentorship for medicine. Medtora bridges this gap with AI-assisted guidance, role-based matching, and a structured platform for meaningful connections. The app combines chat, progress tracking, and community support to make mentorship scalable and impactful.

“Mentorship doubles persistence in medicine for underrepresented groups, yet access remains limited.” — JAMA, 2020

Problem Statement

Minority, first-gen, and immigrant students lack structured mentorship and networks for medical career guidance. Mentors want to help but lack organized tools for connecting, scheduling, and tracking mentee progress.

User Personas

First-Gen Pre-Med Student

Seeks mentorship to navigate applications, prerequisites, and career paths without familial guidance in medicine.

Mentors (Students, Residents, Physicians)

Want to give back efficiently, with tools to manage mentee progress and schedule interactions.

Immigrant Student

Needs guidance on cultural, language, and networking challenges in U.S. medical education.

Career Changer

Requires mentorship tailored to leveraging prior experience and meeting medical school prerequisites.

Practicing Physician Mentor

Desires an easy-to-use platform to mentor the next generation without institutional barriers.

The Solution

Medtora provides a comprehensive mentorship platform combining AI-driven matching, progress tracking, and community features. The product demonstrates my end-to-end capabilities in designing, prototyping, coding, and iterating with AI-assisted tools.

AI Companion

Guides mentees in conversation, suggests resources, and assists in scheduling sessions.

Community Forum

An anonymous space for mentees to ask sensitive questions and share experiences safely.

Role-Based Accounts

Separate onboarding ensures mentors and mentees access features tailored to their needs.

Rich Profiles & Smart Matching

Profiles showcase education, career goals, and specialties; AI matching connects mentees to mentors with shared interests and backgrounds.

Future Features

Integrated calendar, video calls, and multilingual support to enhance accessibility and engagement.

Key Insights & Metrics

  • Mentorship increases medical school acceptance rates by 25% (AAMC, 2021).
  • First-gen students are 2x more likely to succeed with consistent mentorship (JAMA, 2020).
  • AI-assisted platforms improve scalability and reduce barriers (MedEdPublish, 2022).
  • Demo version connected 2 mentees with mentors, validating the concept and potential.

Market & Business Opportunities

50,000+ U.S. pre-med applicants annually require structured mentorship. Medtora can scale via partnerships with medical schools, nonprofits, and student orgs, enabling global access to mentorship and structured guidance.

Prototype & Screens

Sample screens highlight the user experience and end-to-end design.

Tech Stack & AI Tools Used

Built with Swift (iOS), Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage), Node.js backend, and Figma for design.

AI & Vibe Coding Tools: GPT-4, Cursor, Goose AI, Replit, Uizard, and other prototyping accelerators.

End-to-end development included product design, UI/UX, coding, AI integration, and iterative testing.

Reflection & Next Steps

Medtora demonstrated how AI and structured design can amplify mentorship impact. Early demos validated connections, and scaling with calendar integration, video support, and partnerships will extend accessibility for underrepresented students worldwide.

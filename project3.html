Medtora — AI-Powered Mentorship & Support for Aspiring Medical Professionals

Product Design, AI Integration, Full-Stack Development

Case Study • 2025

Setting the Scene

First-generation and underrepresented students often struggle to access the mentorship and guidance they need to enter medicine. Medtora was created to bridge this gap—using AI and community-driven features to make mentorship structured, accessible, and personalized. The app reimagines mentorship by combining chat, scheduling, and AI support into a single platform.

“Mentorship doubles persistence in medicine for underrepresented groups, yet access is still limited.” — JAMA, 2020

Problem Statement

Breaking into medicine is especially challenging for minorities, first-generation, and immigrant students who lack built-in networks. At the same time, mentors want to give back but often don’t have streamlined ways to connect, track progress, and guide students effectively.

User Personas

First-Gen Pre-Med Student

Seeking structured mentorship to understand applications, prerequisites, and career paths without family connections in medicine.

Mentors (Students, Residents, Physicians)

Want to give back and support the next generation but need a simple, organized way to do so.

Immigrant Student

Faces language and cultural barriers while navigating U.S. medical education and exams; mentorship provides clarity.

Career Changer

Needs tailored guidance on prerequisites and applications to leverage prior experience in medicine.

Practicing Physician Mentor

Wants to mentor future doctors but lacks a convenient platform to connect with students outside of institutions.

The Solution

Medtora provides a dedicated platform for mentorship with AI-assisted matching, progress tracking, and built-in community support. By combining human connection with AI tools, the platform ensures mentorship is meaningful, scalable, and impactful.

AI Companion

Supports conversations, moderates chats, and helps with scheduling meetings directly in the app.

Community Forum

An anonymous space for mentees to ask sensitive questions and share experiences.

Role-Based Accounts

Mentors and mentees see features tailored to their needs with separate onboarding flows.

Rich Profiles

Showcase education, specialties, goals, and backgrounds for better matching.

Smart Matching

AI-powered suggestions connect mentees with mentors who share specialties, goals, and backgrounds.

Future Features

Integrated calendar, video chat, and multilingual support to expand accessibility.

Key Research Statistics

  • Mentorship increases medical school acceptance rates by 25% (AAMC, 2021).
  • First-gen students are 2x more likely to succeed with consistent mentorship (JAMA, 2020).
  • AI-assisted platforms improve scale and reduce barriers in career guidance (MedEdPublish, 2022).

Market & Business Opportunities

With 50,000+ annual U.S. pre-med applicants, demand for structured mentorship is immense. Medtora could scale through partnerships with medical schools, nonprofits, and student orgs, while providing accessible mentorship globally.

Next steps include adding scheduling, video calls, and expanding through institutional partnerships to scale mentorship opportunities for underrepresented students in medicine worldwide.

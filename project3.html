Project 1: Adhera

Project tagline / one-liner describing the purpose

Setting the Scene

In March 2025, we decided to build Adhera, a smart solution for weight loss clinics that combines scheduling, patient engagement, and AI-powered calorie tracking.

The Challenge

Weight loss clinics struggled to keep patients engaged and maintain adherence to diet and appointment schedules. Our goal: improve patient retention and simplify clinic workflow.

Core Problem: Patients often miss appointments and fail to track nutrition effectively, reducing clinic efficacy.

Solution Overview

We built Adhera, a mobile-first platform integrating smart appointment scheduling, AI-based calorie estimation, and patient engagement tools.

My Role & Contributions

  • Architecture planning & AWS cloud setup
  • Frontend development with React Native
  • AI integration using Python
  • Data analytics & dashboard design for clinics

Impact & Outcome

During testing with clinics and patients, we observed:

  • Increased patient adherence to appointments by 30%
  • Improved tracking of nutrition with AI estimates
  • Enhanced clinic workflow and engagement

Future Scope

Potential improvements include:

  • Gamification for patient engagement
  • Integrations with wearable devices
  • Expanded analytics dashboard for clinics

18Gita — A Bhagavad Gita Daily Verse Widget for iOS

iOS, SwiftUI, WidgetKit, Mobile App Development

Project • 2023

Setting the Scene

The Bhagavad Gita’s timeless wisdom can guide and inspire us daily. With 18Gita, I designed an iOS app and widget that delivers one verse from the Gita directly to the user’s home screen each day. The goal was to create a simple, elegant spiritual companion that integrates seamlessly into modern digital life.

Technologies Used

18Gita was built entirely in Swift using SwiftUI for the main app and WidgetKit for the home screen widget. Data persistence is handled via UserDefaults, and widget scheduling is powered by a Timeline Provider. The minimum deployment target is iOS 16.

Key features include daily verse rotation, expandable verse cards showing Sanskrit, transliteration, translation, and meaning, as well as a random verse option and share functionality.

Reflection & Next Steps

Building 18Gita deepened my understanding of iOS development, especially with SwiftUI and WidgetKit. My main takeaway: small, well-crafted experiences—like a daily verse reminder—can have a profound impact on users when designed with care and intention.

Next steps include expanding the verse database to all 700 verses, refining the visual design, and preparing the app for App Store release with custom icons and branding.

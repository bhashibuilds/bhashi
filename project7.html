Sprouted — A To-Do List That Grows With You

Desktop App, Productivity, Gamification, Creative Coding

Project • 2024

Setting the Scene

Staying productive can be difficult, but motivation grows when we see progress. Sprouted is a desktop to-do list app that rewards completed tasks with a growing digital plant. Each task is tied to a timer—when you finish on time, your plant flourishes. If not, the plant withers away. The goal was to design a tool that combines productivity and mindfulness through gentle gamification.

Technologies Used

Sprouted was developed as a cross-platform desktop app using Python and PyQt for the interface. The task timer logic was handled with Python’s time and threading libraries, while SQLite was used for lightweight task persistence. Plant animations were created with custom SVGs rendered dynamically in the UI.

The app was packaged into a standalone executable using PyInstaller for easy distribution.

Reflection & Next Steps

Building Sprouted helped me explore gamification in productivity tools and how visual feedback can shape user behavior. My biggest takeaway: small, playful rewards (like a thriving plant) encourage users to stay consistent without feeling pressured.

Next steps include adding plant variety, streak tracking, and integrating with calendar apps for a smoother workflow. I also plan to experiment with mobile ports so users can take their plants with them.
